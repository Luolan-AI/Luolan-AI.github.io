About / Research

Portrait your photo / coming soon

My main direction is AI/ML and representation learning, with a focus on long-context processing, information compression, retrieval, and reasoning.

As a secondary interest, I explore computational cognitive science and NeuroAI to understand memory, attention, and reasoning in human cognition. I am interested in the boundary where machine representations and human cognitive mechanisms begin to illuminate one another.
